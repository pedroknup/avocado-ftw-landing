About us

Pedro Knup - Founder

Hello there, I'm Pedro, a Brazilian software engineer based in the Netherlands. Let me introduce you to AvocadoFTW, a passion project that was born out of my personal need for a simple and effective meal planning and macro tracking app.

When I first started this project, I named it AvocadoFTW - "Avocado For the Win." It might seem nonsensical, but there's something about it that resonates with me. Perhaps it's the cheeky avocado logo flexing its muscles or simply because I've grown fond of the name over time.

Initially created for my own use, AvocadoFTW reflects how serious I was about developing a tool that would make meal planning and macro tracking easy and efficient. As the sole developer behind this endeavor, I set out to build an app that would meet my specific needs and preferences.

As word spread among my friends and network, requests for access to AvocadoFTW started pouring in. This prompted me to enhance the app's features and capabilities to cater to a growing user base. Transitioning from a basic spreadsheet setup to a full-fledged platform with its own backend, database, and client marked a significant milestone in the evolution of AvocadoFTW.

I decided to make AvocadoFTW an open-source project because my goal was never about monetization; it was always about providing a valuable tool for users like you. However, maintaining the app comes with substantial server-related costs that I bear to ensure its continued availability and performance.

Join me on this journey towards simplified meal planning, effortless macro tracking, and consistent healthy habits with AvocadoFTW. Together, we can make healthy living more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
