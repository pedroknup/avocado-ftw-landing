Frequently Asked Question

What is Avocado FTW?

Avocado FTW is a simple meal planner and diet tracker app that helps you keep track of your meals and macros. It's designed to be easy to use and stress-free, so you can focus on your health and fitness goals.

How do I get early access?

You can request early access by signing up for our beta program. Just fill out the form above and we'll send you a login code when we're ready to launch.

Is Avocado FTW free to use?

Yes, Avocado FTW is free to use. We believe that everyone should have access to tools that help them live a healthier life, so we're committed to keeping the app free for everyone, and open source

What platforms is Avocado FTW available on?

Avocado FTW will be available on iOS, Android, and the web. You can use it on your phone, tablet, or computer, so you can track your meals and macros wherever you are.
